# Core Concept

ABOVE / BELOW is an asymmetric multiplayer PvP game built around modern drone warfare, vulnerable operators, and layered battlefield control.

One team controls drones from hidden ground positions.

The other team fights to survive the drones while locating and eliminating the operators controlling them.

The game is designed around two simultaneous battlefields:

* the air war above
* the pilot hunt below

The defining mechanic is that pilots remain physically present in the match while operating drones.

Destroying the operator can disable or crash the linked drone.

---

# Core Design Pillars

## 1. Human Vulnerability Behind Technology

The strongest battlefield presence is attached to a vulnerable human player somewhere on the map.

This creates:

* tension
* paranoia
* relocation gameplay
* protection dynamics
* information warfare
* asymmetric risk

---

## 2. Readable Counterplay

Every drone and class should have:

* strengths
* weaknesses
* soft counters
* situational dominance

The game should avoid:

* unavoidable kills
* hard-lock metas
* "best pick" dominance

Players should feel like they are choosing answers to enemy behavior.

---

## 3. Two Connected Battlefields

The drone war and ground war must constantly affect each other.

Examples:

* soldiers forcing pilot relocation
* drones exposing ground movement
* EMPs interrupting dives
* hidden pilots collapsing air control after being discovered

The battlefield should feel interconnected, not separated.

---

## 4. Accessible Tactical Gameplay

The game should feel tactical without becoming:

* milsim-heavy
* inaccessible
* overly realistic
* simulation-first

The priority is:

* tension
* readability
* memorable situations
* replayable team dynamics

# Team Structure

## ABOVE: Pilot Team

Pilots operate linked drones while remaining physically vulnerable on the ground.

### Current Drone Variants

#### GPS Drone

Role:

* stable overwatch
* battlefield pressure
* long-range visibility

Weakness:

* vulnerable to RF jamming

---

#### FPV Drone

Role:

* fast dive pressure
* burst attack runs
* punishing isolated players

Weakness:

* vulnerable to EMP and close-range denial

---

#### Fiber-Optic FPV Drone

Role:

* jammer-resistant pressure
* anti-Counter-UAV disruption

Weakness:

* visible tether
* vulnerable to gunfire
* lower mobility ceiling than FPV

---

## BELOW: Soldier Team

Soldiers survive drone pressure while hunting operators.

### Current Soldier Classes

#### Assault

Role:

* general combat pressure
* anti-Fiber gunplay
* adaptable frontline role

Equipment:

* rifle
* chaff grenade

---

#### Counter-UAV

Role:

* anti-GPS control
* RF suppression
* area denial

Equipment:

* directional jammer
* frag grenade

---

#### Heavy

Role:

* anti-dive defense
* close-range anchor
* EMP shutdown pressure

Equipment:

* shotgun
* EMP grenade

# Core Counter Triangle

| Threat | Primary Counter | Gameplay Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| GPS Drone | Counter-UAV | RF suppression |
| FPV Drone | Heavy | EMP + close denial |
| Fiber Drone | Assault | direct gun pressure |
| Heavy | GPS | ranged pressure |
| Assault | FPV | aggressive dives |
| Counter-UAV | Fiber | jammer bypass |

The triangle should remain readable to casual players while rewarding coordinated team composition.

# One-Sentence Pitch

ABOVE / BELOW is an asymmetric multiplayer shooter where drone pilots control the sky while soldiers hunt the vulnerable operators below.

---

# Short Pitch

Pilots fly GPS, FPV, and fiber-optic drones from hidden ground positions.

Soldiers fight back with rifles, jammers, EMPs, chaff, and teamwork.

The drones are dangerous.

The pilots are not untouchable.

---

# The Core Hook

Every drone has a human behind it.

Pilots dominate the battlefield from above, but somewhere on the map their real body is still exposed.

If soldiers find the operator:
the drone goes down too.

That creates the game's central tension:

The sky belongs to the drone.
Survival happens below it.

---

# What Makes It Different

## Two Connected Battlefields

The fight happens in two layers:

### ABOVE

Drones scout, pressure, dive, and track movement.

### BELOW

Soldiers survive the sky while hunting the people controlling it.

Winning one layer changes the other instantly.

---

## Every Role Has A Counter

* Jammers stop vulnerable drones.
* Fiber drones ignore RF disruption.
* EMPs punish aggressive dives.
* Rifles bring down what jammers cannot stop.
* Pilots relocate.
* Soldiers track them.

The game is built around pressure, adaptation, and fast tactical decisions.
